## Executive Summary

**Market Reality:**
Ballarat web design market is saturated with agencies offering identical positioning: "custom-built websites, local knowledge, full-service, 15-20 years experience."

**Strategic Opportunity:**
Seven critical voice gaps exist where no competitor owns positioning. Brighter Websites can dominate through:
1. AI-ready SEO positioning (strongest gap)
2. Framework-based methodology (proprietary IP)
3. Offline-sale business specialization (niche clarity)
4. Measurable proof mechanisms (revenue outcomes, not portfolio volume)

**Competitive Strategy:**
Track 3 primary competitors (Stoke, Seamer, Gasoline) while exploiting voice gaps through ALTC-driven content strategy.
